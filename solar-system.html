Solar System

Through fun hands-on experiences, unique objects, and stunning imagery, this exhibition allowed visitors to discover the incredible story of our closest star—the Sun.

Set at the centre of our solar system, the Suns brilliant light shapes our sense of time, our health and our environment. People have tried to harness its power and uncover its secrets since the dawn of civilisation.

From 3,000-year-old artefacts to upcoming space missions and even a nuclear fusion reactor, this exhibition took visitors on a visual, action-packed journey that explored the fascinating story of humankinds relationship with our closest star.
