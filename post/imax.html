IMAX: THE RONSON THEATRE

Immerse yourself in the big screen.

Escape the hustle and bustle of the museum and settle in for an unforgettable experience in our IMAX cinema.

Boasting a next-generation IMAX® Laser 4K projector along with our much-loved IMAX 70mm film projector, our cinema is a truly unique experience for all filmgoers. This means we’re one of only two screens in Europe to feature the best of digital and analogue cinema, and are equipped to show films in all their cinematic glory.

Our IMAX theatre can host newly released blockbusters, stunning 3D documentary films and cinema classics as well as an array of live events. Also, be sure to get yourself some snacks and drinks from our concessions area.

To be the first to know when our next blockbuster goes on sale, register your interest by filling out this form.
