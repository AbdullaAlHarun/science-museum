Visit and Tickets

To ensure that you have a safe and enjoyable visit, please arrive at our main entrance within your chosen time slot. Latecomers and those without a ticket may not be admitted. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult (18+).

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

05

Food and drink

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

06

Shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you have left the museum.

Explore our collection beyond the museum floor and find out the fascinating histories behind even the smallest objects.
